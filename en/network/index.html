History
of the firm.

«ALBERTO E. GUERRA P. LAW OFFICE» was established in the Republic of Panama in 1987. Until 2020 it operated alongside the firm GUERRA, SIERRA & ATIE; since then it has practiced independently, preserving the network of international correspondents built over three decades.

In 2020 the firm created the International Sports Business Department in alliance with the Spanish office Crespo & Ruiz. It is the country's only unit specialized in international sports law with direct representation before bodies such as the WBC, FIFA, and the Olympic Committee.
